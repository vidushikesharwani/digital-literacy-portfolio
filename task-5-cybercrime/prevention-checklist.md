# Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist for College Students

## General Online Safety
- [ ] Use strong passwords with letters, numbers, 
      and special characters (e.g., Xk#9mL@2qP)
- [ ] Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all 
      important accounts
- [ ] Never click on unknown links received via 
      WhatsApp, SMS or email
- [ ] Verify website URLs before entering any 
      personal information — look for "https://"
- [ ] Do not share OTP, password or UPI PIN with 
      anyone — not even bank officials
- [ ] Regularly update your phone and app software 
      to fix security vulnerabilities
- [ ] Use only official app stores (Google Play / 
      Apple App Store) to download apps

## UPI & Financial Safety (Important for Students!)
- [ ] **Never scan a QR code to RECEIVE money** — 
      QR codes are only for sending money, not receiving
- [ ] Never share your UPI PIN with anyone claiming 
      to be customer support — real companies never ask for it
- [ ] Always verify the recipient's UPI ID before 
      confirming any payment

## Social Media Safety
- [ ] Keep your social media profiles private
- [ ] Never post your phone number, address, or 
      financial details publicly
- [ ] Do not accept friend requests from unknown people

## If You Are A Victim — Report Immediately!
- [ ]  Visit: **cybercrime.gov.in**
- [ ]  Call: **1930** (National Cyber Crime Helpline)
- [ ] Report to your bank within 24 hours for 
      maximum chance of recovery
