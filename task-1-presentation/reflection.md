# Task 1 — Reflection

## Tool Used
I used Canva to create my digital literacy infographic. 
Canva is a free, browser-based design tool that offers 
ready-made templates, icons, and layouts.

## What My Design Shows
My infographic titled "Digital Literacy: A Student's Guide" 
covers three key topics:
- **What is Digital Literacy** — definition and importance
- **Top Digital Tools for Students** — Google Drive, Canva, 
  Notion, Grammarly, and more
- **Safe Internet Practices** — strong passwords, 2FA, 
  avoiding phishing links

## One Thing I Found Difficult / Interesting
The most interesting part of this task was learning how 
to communicate complex information visually. Choosing the 
right icons and colour scheme to make the infographic 
engaging yet informative was a creative challenge.

The most difficult part was fitting all three topics onto 
a single page without making it look cluttered. It taught 
me that simplicity and clarity are the most important 
principles of good visual design.
