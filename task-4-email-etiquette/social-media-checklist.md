# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## Do's
1. Keep your profile professional and up to date
2. Think before you post: ask "would I show this to my professor?"
3. Use privacy settings to control who sees your content
4. Give credit when sharing someone else's work
5. Engage positively: support peers and spread helpful content
6. Follow industry leaders and learn from their posts
7. Use LinkedIn to build your professional network

## Don'ts
1. Don't share personal information like address or phone number publicly
2. Don't post offensive, hateful or discriminatory content
3. Don't engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
4. Don't share fake news or unverified information
5. Don't post confidential information about your college or peers
6. Don't use social media during exams or important deadlines
7. Don't ignore privacy settings : review them regularly
